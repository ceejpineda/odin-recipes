Chicken Wings Recipe

Hickory Smoked Chicken Wings

Tender hickory-smoked chicken wings without the wait.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds frozen chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • hickory wood chips
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2 Place chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  3. Step 3 Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
  4. Step 4 Combine paprika, chili powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl. Coat baked chicken wings with the dry rub. Place wings in the smoker with the hickory wood chips.
  5. Step 5 Smoke chicken for 1 hour at 225 degrees F (110 degrees C).
  6. Step 6 Transfer smoked wings to a large bowl and toss with barbeque sauce.
